\section{Introduction}
This document presents the procedure and results of Total Ionizing Dose (TID) tests of 32Gb NAND Flash memories from Micron (MT29F32G08ABAAAWP-ITZ:A). This model was chosen as it is expected to be used in the PW-Sat3 satellite, developed by Students' Space Association at Warsaw University of Technology.

The tests were conducted from 20.10.2025 to 31.10.2025 in the Co-60 facility at the European Space Agency's (ESA) European Space Research and Technology Centre (ESTEC) in Noordwijk, Netherlands.

\section{Facility}
The Co-60 facility at ESTEC is an ISO17025-accredited gamma radiation testing and calibration laboratory.
